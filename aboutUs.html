ABOUT US


Welcome to our photography website, we are passionate photographers who believe in the power of sharing knowledge to inspire others.


The goal is to create a space where amateur photographers can learn, grow, and connect in a supportive environment.


This website is a place for you to showcase your photos, receive feedback via a member photo rating system, and to be inspired by other peoples photos, we are building a community on a foundation of respect and honesty and we expect everyone to be polite and thoughtful.


We ask that all photos uploaded are your own original work.


Whether you're just starting out or have been taking photos for a while, we invite you to join us, share your work, see what others are creating, and let's help each other become better photographers.


To join our newsletter, click the news letter button and leave your email address, our newsletter is published yearly and will be emailed to you.


If you would like assistance, click on the Assistance button, leave your phone number and we will contact you.
